Do-It-Yourself
(Optional) To make the changes yourself in DSMapStudio
Open your regulation.bin file with DSMapStudio and find Param "SpEffectParam"
Navigate to row 310000 to find the Crimson Amber Medallion.
Change the "Absorption %:" fields to 0.
Save changes and start game.
